robinhood_agentic — the boundaries

What it is: the ward (local bound) + the meter (hosted signed read) wrapped around an agentic trading loop, so an operator can see whether the agent trades the same when it believes nobody is reviewing the session.

What it is not: a broker, a signal, or advice. The harness is mock-only until an operator sentence authorizes a live broker and names the account + window. That guard is in the code, not just this page.

Score-blind, as everywhere: the read prices flat and no payment can move it. The honest-scope card ships on every response.
